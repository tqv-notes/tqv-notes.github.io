Scattering Transform (ST) is a simplified version of Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) with regourous mathematical foundations. It was originated from signal/image processing fields ([S. Mallat](https://arxiv.org/pdf/1101.2286) and [J. Bruna & S. Mallat](https://arxiv.org/pdf/1203.1513)) and found applications in diverse tasks: image classification, finance, astrophysics, material science, ...
